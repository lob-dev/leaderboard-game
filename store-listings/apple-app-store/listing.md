**Description:**
The Leaderboard Game flips everything you know about gaming on its head. There are no hidden menus, no loading screens, no separate scoreboards. The leaderboard IS the game.

Your mission is simple: climb the ranks. Every tap earns you points. Every point moves you up. Watch yourself rise in real-time as you compete against other players for the top spot.

Features:
• The leaderboard is front and center — always visible, always live
• Simple one-tap gameplay anyone can pick up instantly
• Real-time competition — watch your rank change as you play
• Clean, modern UI designed for pure competitive focus
• Addictive "just one more tap" gameplay loop

How high can you climb? Download now and find out.
