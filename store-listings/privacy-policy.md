# Privacy Policy — Leaderboard Game

**Last Updated:** February 24, 2026

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Leaderboard Game ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Player Name:** A display name you choose for the leaderboard

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Score Data:** Your game scores for leaderboard ranking
- **Device Information:** Basic device type and operating system version for compatibility purposes

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal contact information (email, phone, address)
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect financial information
- We do not use advertising trackers
- We do not sell any user data

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:
- Display your name and score on the in-game leaderboard
- Maintain leaderboard rankings
- Ensure app compatibility and performance

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

## Data Retention

Your leaderboard data is retained as long as you use the App. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App is rated for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the App.
